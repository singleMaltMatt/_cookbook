Sushi Rice

This is a simple recipe for perfect sushi rice.

This recipe can be used when making sushi or simply to make sticky short-grain rice for other Asian dishes.

Should you wish to use this recipe only for sticky short-gran rice; do not proceed to Step 2.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Short-Grain Rice
  • 3 Cups Water
  • ½ Cup Rice Vinegar
  • 1 Tablespoon Vegetable Oil
  • ¼ Cup White Sugar
  • 1 Teaspoon Salt

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Rinse the rice in a strainer or colander until the water runs clear.

    Combine with water in a medium saucepan.

    Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover and cook for 20 minutes.

    Rice should be tender and water should be absorbed. Cool until cool enough to handle.

  2. Step 2

    3. In a small saucepan, combine the rice vinegar, oil, sugar and salt.

    Cook over medium heat until the sugar dissolves.

    Cool, then stir into the cooked rice.

    When you pour this in to the rice it will seem very wet.

    Keep stirring and the rice will dry as it cools.
