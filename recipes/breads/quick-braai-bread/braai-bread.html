Quick Braai Bread

A simple braai bread to impress your guests.

That's it!

Ingredients

  • 15ml (1 Tbsp) oil
  • 1 large onion - chopped
  • 500g self-raising flour
  • 2.5ml (½tsp) salt
  • 500ml (2 cups) buttermilk/yoghurt
  • 60ml (4 Tbsp) chutney
  • 1 medium tomato - sliced
  • 125ml (½ cup cheddar cheese - grated

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Pre-heat oven to 180°C.

  2. Step 2

    3. Heat oil and fry onion until golden brown.

  3. Step 3

    4. Mix flour and salt.

    Stir in onion and buttermilk/yoghurt to form a soft, sticky dough. (The dough should look failry rough to add character to the bread.)

  4. Step 4

    5. Spoon the dough on a lightly greased baking tray and press to form a 22cm round.

    Spread chutney over top, arrange tomato slices and sprinkle cheese over.

  5. Step 5

    6. Bake in a pre-heated oven at 180°C for 40 minutes.

  6. Serve

    7. Serve warm with butter.


    This recipe can be halved if a smaller bread is preferred - forming an 18cm round and baking for 30 minutes.
