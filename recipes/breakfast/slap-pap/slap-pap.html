Slap Pap

Slap Pap, a traditional South African porridge breakfast known to many families, still loved to this day.

This simple recipe only takes 30 minutes, and will guarantee that you will go back for seconds!

Many varients exist for this recipe, but the basis remains the same.

Feel free to add whatever you like to your bowl, the most popular choices are sugar, milk, honey, berries and butter.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Maize Meal
  • 2 Pinches Salt
  • 4 Cups Water

Directions

  1. Prepare Your Maize

    2. Place 1 cup Maize Meal in a small bowl, add 1 cup Water, stir until clump free and let rest for about 10 minutes

    Note: For best results, leave water and maize mixture overnight to soak and soften, this reduces the cooking time, however this is completely optional, letting the maize soak for a few minutes is more than enough.

  2. Heat The Stove

    3. Place a small sauce pan on the stove and set the temperature to High.

    Pour the remaining 3 cups Water in the sauce pan and add Salt.


    When your water starts to boil, immediately turn the heat down to Low.


    Add your Maize mixture to the sauce pan, stir until completely clump free.

  3. Cook

    4. Close the sauce pan with lid and let cook for 20 - 30 minutes

  4. Serve

    5. Serve hot with milk and sugar, alternatively butter and sugar
